Within just three months, I successfully delivered a fully functional, end-to-end solution that seamlessly integrated with the company‚Äôs workflows.

To ensure transparency and user trust, I incorporated Responsible AI mechanisms, providing clear justifications for product recommendations and aligning the solution with the company‚Äôs ethical standards.

This modular chatbot system not only addressed the company’s immediate needs but also offered flexibility for future customizations and scalability.
